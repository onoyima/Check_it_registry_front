1. User-Facing Core Application Screens:
Screen Title: Device Check-In / Scanning
•	Context: Check It - Device Registry & Recovery System mobile app
•	Components & Functions:
•	Input Field (IMEI/Serial Number): Allows users (likely buyers or sellers during a transaction) to enter a device's unique identifier.
•	"Scan" Button: Initiates the check. When pressed, the system automatically captures the MAC and IP addresses of the checking device and forces live location tracking for the report.
•	Warning Message: A prominent text area that advises users that only the seller of the product should perform the check, preferably with their own device and account details, to ensure proper ownership transfer.
•	Overall Layout: A modern, secure aesthetic guiding the user through the initial verification step.
Screen Title: Device Check Report
•	Context: Check It - Device Registry & Recovery System mobile app
•	Components & Functions:
•	Device Status Display: Shows the current registration status of the checked device (e.g., "Verified," "Stolen," "Pending Verification").
•	Captured Data: Clearly displays the MAC and IP addresses of the device used for checking.
•	Live Location Display: Pinpoints and shows the live geographical location where the check was performed.
•	Checker Details: Shows the name and account information of the logged-in user who performed the device check.
•	Summary: Consolidates all collected information into a report format.
•	Action Buttons: "Confirm and Submit Report" (to law enforcement) and "Go Back" options for user control.
•	Purpose: To generate a comprehensive record of a device check, especially for reporting to law enforcement.
Screen Title: Ownership Transfer Initiation

•	Security Emphasis: The design highlights that the process is secure and may involve an OTP verification step sent to the seller or new owner.
•	Purpose: To facilitate a secure and documented transfer of device ownership.
